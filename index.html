Shift at Midnight FAQ

How do I start playing?

Download the demo on Itch.io or wishlist it on Steam, then click 'Start Playing' to begin your first night.

Is Shift at Midnight free?

Yes, the demo on Itch.io is free. The full version will be paid and available on Steam.

Can I play with friends?

Definitely! Co‑op supports 1–3 players working together to survive the night shift.

What platforms is it on?

The game is currently on PC via Itch.io and coming soon to Steam; Mac support is planned.

How do I improve my skills?

Practice running efficient interrogations, manage barricades smartly, and adapt to each night’s challenges.
